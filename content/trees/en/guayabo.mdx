## Physical Description

### Overall Form

Guava is a small, spreading tree or large shrub with distinctive smooth bark that peels in patches. It typically has multiple stems and a dense, rounded crown. The tree is semi-evergreen, retaining leaves year-round in humid areas but dropping some in dry seasons.

### Identifying Features

<TwoColumn>
<Column>

#### Bark and Trunk

- **Bark color**: Green to reddish-brown
- **Texture**: SMOOTH, peeling in patches
- **Pattern**: Mottled, reveals underlayers
- **Trunk**: Often multi-stemmed
- **Very distinctive**: Easily recognized bark

#### Leaves

- **Type**: Simple, opposite
- **Shape**: Elliptical to oblong
- **Size**: 7-15 cm long
- **Veins**: Very prominent beneath
- **Texture**: Leathery, pubescent below
- **Aromatic**: Crushed leaves fragrant

</Column>
<Column>

#### Flowers

- **Type**: Solitary or few in axils
- **Color**: White
- **Size**: 2-3 cm diameter
- **Stamens**: Numerous, showy
- **Fragrance**: Pleasant, sweet
- **Timing**: Year-round with peaks

#### Fruit

- **Type**: Berry
- **Shape**: Round to pear-shaped
- **Size**: 4-10 cm depending on variety
- **Skin**: Yellow-green when ripe
- **Flesh**: White to deep pink
- **Seeds**: Numerous, hard, embedded
- **Aroma**: VERY fragrant when ripe

</Column>
</TwoColumn>

## Cultivation

### Growing Guava

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Site Requirements">
    **Ideal Conditions**: - Full sun (some shade tolerated) - Well-drained soil
    - Tropical to warm subtropical climate - Frost-free location - Regular water
    (drought tolerant once established) **Soil**: - Very adaptable - pH 4.5-8.2
    (wide range!) - Tolerates poor soils - Benefits from organic matter
  </AccordionItem>

<AccordionItem title="Propagation">
  **From Seed**: - Easy germination - Variable offspring (hybridization) - 3-4
  years to fruit - Good for rootstocks **Vegetative** (recommended for
  varieties): - Air layering (common method) - Cuttings (with rooting hormone) -
  Grafting (for specific cultivars) - Fruit in 1-2 years
</AccordionItem>

  <AccordionItem title="Care and Harvest">
    **Maintenance**: - Prune to shape and control size - Fertilize with balanced
    fertilizer - Water regularly when young - Mulch around base **Harvest**: -
    Fruit mature in 3-4 months from flowering - Harvest when yellow-green, still
    firm - Ripen at room temperature - Very short shelf life when ripe
    **Yield**: - Productive tree: 50-150 kg/year - Multiple crops possible in
    tropics
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>
