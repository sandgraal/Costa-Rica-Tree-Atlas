# Cerillo

<Callout type="success" title="The Swamp Specialist with Red Blood">
  The **Cerillo** (_Symphonia globulifera_) is one of Costa Rica's most
  distinctive wetland trees, immediately recognizable by its stilt roots and
  brilliant red latex that bleeds when cut. Found in swamps, flooded forests,
  and along waterways, this tree has been used for centuries to make dugout
  canoes and in traditional medicine.
</Callout>
