<AccordionItem title="ðŸ”¬ Taxonomic Notes">

_Vochysia_ is a Neotropical genus with about 140 species. The species epithet _guatemalensis_ refers to Guatemala, where the type specimen was collected.

**Related Species**: _V. ferruginea_ (Botarrama) is the other major Costa Rican reforestation species in this genus. Chancho Blanco has larger leaves, whiter flowers, and grows at higher elevations.

**Growth Champion**: Among native Central American trees, Chancho Blanco has one of the highest documented growth rates, making it economically competitive with exotic species like _Gmelina_ and _Tectona_.

</AccordionItem>
