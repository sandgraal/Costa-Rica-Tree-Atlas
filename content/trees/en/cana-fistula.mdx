The Ca√±a F√≠stula is a medium-sized deciduous tree with an open, spreading crown. ### Distinctive Features

<TwoColumn>
<Column>

#### Leaves

- **Type**: Pinnately compound
- **Leaflets**: 4-8 pairs, opposite
- **Size**: 7-12 cm per leaflet
- **Shape**: Ovate to elliptic
- **Texture**: Smooth, slightly glossy
- **Deciduous**: Drops leaves before flowering

#### Bark

- **Young**: Smooth, pale gray-green
- **Mature**: Rough, grayish-brown
- **Texture**: Becomes fissured with age
- **Inner Bark**: Reddish

</Column>
<Column>

#### The Golden Flowers

- **Type**: Showy, 5 petals
- **Color**: Bright golden yellow
- **Size**: 4-7 cm diameter
- **Arrangement**: Long pendant racemes (30-60 cm!)
- **Fragrance**: Mildly sweet
- **Duration**: 2-3 weeks per tree
- **Season**: Late dry season (March-May in CR)

#### Seed Pods

- **Shape**: Long cylindrical tubes
- **Length**: 30-60 cm (up to 90 cm!)
- **Diameter**: 2-2.5 cm
- **Color**: Dark brown to black when ripe
- **Contents**: Sweet pulp surrounding seeds
- **Persistence**: Hang on tree for months

</Column>
</TwoColumn>

## Propagation

### Growing from Seed

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Seed Collection">
    **When to Collect**: - Pods mature 6-12 months after flowering - Dark
    brown/black pods are ready - Pods should rattle when shaken **Processing**:
    - Break open woody pods - Separate seeds from sticky pulp - Clean and dry
    seeds - Store in cool, dry place
  </AccordionItem>

<AccordionItem title="Germination">
  **Treatment Required**: - Seeds have hard coat - Scarify: Nick with file or
  sandpaper - OR: Soak in hot water overnight - OR: Sulfuric acid treatment
  (professional) **Planting**: - Plant 2 cm deep - Keep moist but not wet -
  Germination: 1-4 weeks - Transplant when 15-20 cm tall
</AccordionItem>
