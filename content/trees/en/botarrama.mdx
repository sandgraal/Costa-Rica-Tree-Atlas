# Botarrama

<Callout type="success" title="The Forest Restorer">
  **Botarrama** (_Vochysia ferruginea_), also known as **Mayo** or **Chancho
  Colorado**, is one of the most successful native reforestation species in
  Costa Rica. This fast-growing pioneer rapidly colonizes degraded lands,
  creating the forest structure that allows slower-growing species to establish.
</Callout>

_Vochysia_ is a Neotropical genus of about 140 species, most diverse in South America. The species epithet _ferruginea_ refers to the rusty-brown color of the bark and young twigs.

**Related Species**: _V. guatemalensis_ (Chancho Blanco) is the other major Costa Rican reforestation species in this genus. Both are fast-growing pioneers but occupy slightly different niches.
