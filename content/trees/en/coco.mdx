# Coco (Coconut Palm)

<Callout type="success" title="The Tree of Life">
  **Coco** (_Cocos nucifera_) is often called the "Tree of Life" because every
  part of the palm can be used. Along Costa Rica's beautiful coastlines, coconut
  palms define the tropical landscape and provide essential resources to coastal
  communities.
