<AccordionItem title="ðŸ”¬ Taxonomic Notes">

Costa Rican oaks belong primarily to the red oak section (Lobatae) of genus _Quercus_. They represent the southernmost distribution of oaks in the Americas, with several endemic species found only in Central American highlands.

**Endemic Species**: Several Costa Rican oak species are endemic to the region, including _Q. copeyensis_ and _Q. costaricensis_.

**Evolutionary Significance**: These oaks represent an ancient lineage that migrated south when North and South America connected, adapting to highland conditions.

</AccordionItem>
