# Gallinazo

<Callout type="success" title="The Speed Demon">
  **Gallinazo** (_Schizolobium parahyba_) is nature's skyscraper builder,
  shooting up to 30 meters in just 5-7 years. When this legume giant blooms, its
  crown transforms into a spectacular golden dome visible from kilometers away,
  earning it the nickname "tower of gold."
